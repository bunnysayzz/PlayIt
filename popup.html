Play It

Watch movies & TV shows from multiple sources

How to use:

  1. Go to IMDb: Visit any IMDb movie or TV show page
  2. Find the button: Look for "Play It" above "Add to Watchlist"
  3. Click Play It: Opens streaming interface in new window
  4. Choose source: Select from multiple streaming providers
